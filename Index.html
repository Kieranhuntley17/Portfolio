About Me
-

For the past decade, I've immersed myself in the world of PCs. My journey began with modding games and creating game servers. While many found the issues frustrating, I loved the problem-solving aspect of fixing them.
Since getting my first computer, I've been building PCs and exploring all aspects of computing. From hardware to software, networking to cybersecurity, I've delved into every corner (even graphic design and music production).
Challenges that frustrate others have always excited me, driving my passion for continuous learning and problem solving.
