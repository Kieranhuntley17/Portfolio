About Me

For the past decade, I've immersed myself in the world of PCs. My journey began with modding games and creating game servers. While many found the issues frustrating, I loved the problem-solving aspect of fixing them. Since getting my first computer, I've been building PCs and exploring all aspects of computing. From hardware to software, networking to cybersecurity, I've delved into every corner of this field. Challenges that frustrate others have always excited me, driving my passion for technology and continuous learning. Thanks for visiting my page. Whether you're here for insights, advice, or to share a passion for PCs, I'm excited to connect with you.
